About

infopet.com is our initial step on distributing and acknowledging information on of our most loved family members of all time the dogs.These pets are the most loving and a playful creation of god. Their bond with humans have reached a new zeal over a century.Indeed we are promoting the species and shall look forward to improve human's understanding and bonding with them.Infopet shall be obliged to spread knowledge and recieve feedbacks from our readers for further improvements.
